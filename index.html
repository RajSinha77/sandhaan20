About Sandhaan

SANDHAAN, the annual techno management festival of BIT Sindri. Ever since its inception SANDHAAN has endeavored to live and promote innovation and technical prowess. Over last ten years, it has been a platform for young technocrats of India to showcase and sharpen their skills and prove their talents.

The festival comprises of a number of events from technical, entrepreneurial and management field, including workshops, guest lectures from eminent personalities alongside several social outreach initiatives. With a view of rising global problems and challenges in the direction of life sustainability and providing effective solutions to the issues, the two day long mega tech fest emphasizes on improving the technicalities of the young minds and catapult their potential, which can prove to be an asset to the nation. SANDHAAN targets the technical nerves of the participants. It witnessed participation from over 30 colleges and 100 schools and a footfall of over 8000 students.
